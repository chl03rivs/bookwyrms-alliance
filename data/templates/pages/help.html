About Us

Finding friends in your twenties is tough and that is where BookWyrm's Alliance comes in!

This is a forum for bookworms, aiming to be a sort of "third-place" to meet and interact with like-minded people.

We are planning to add more features in the future, but for now, we've got your basics covered:

  • View posts and comments (no account required)
  • Write your own posts and comments (account required)
  • Personalise your profile to help users get to know you
  • Report any content or user that is creating a hostile environment

Frequently Asked Questions

Here you can list common questions and answers.

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the book icon at the top right of the page and select " Register".

You will need to fill in a brief form with your details. Once registered, you can log in and join the community discussions!

Is the website free to use?

Yes, BookWyrms Alliance is completely free to use. You can access all features like creating posts, commenting, and participating in discussions without any cost.

Please note that we do require the creation of an account in order to publish posts or comments. This is to help our moderation efforts and is also completely free.

How do I reset my password?

If you’ve forgotten your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You’ll receive an email with instructions on how to reset your password.

How can I post in the Community?

Once you're logged in, you have two options:

  1. Go to the Community page
  2. Or, open the Profile menu (top right of any page)

Then, you can click on " New Post".

Fill in the post details and click " Publish Post" to share it with others!

What should I do if I encounter inappropriate content?

If you see inappropriate content/behaviour, please report it by clicking the "Report" button. It looks like:

  • A flag , located right below the post or comment
  • A on the user's profile

Our team will review the report and take action if necessary.
